Contribute To The ESRG Windows® Tool Set

The goal of The ESRG Tool Set is to provide functionality related to the design and construction of embedded systems, for free, and with the advantages of open-source.

We welcome all contributions of tools, all contributions of papers and articles, and all assistance.

If you wish to contribute a tool, here are the restrictions and the expectations:

  • You must be willing and legally able to place the tool under the GPL.
  • You must maintain the tool source code in CVS at SourceForge.
  • You must maintain the documentation for the tool in CVS at SourceForge.  All forms of documentation are welcome, but usually the best approach is to incorporate the documentation into the ESRG microcontroller software book.
  • You must be willing to assume zonal responsibility for the tool (react to bug reports, etc.).
  • You must be willing to interact with CVS directly to make changes, corrections, etc.

If you wish to contribute a paper or article, here are the restrictions and expectations:

  • You must be legally able to place the paper or article on the web and bundle it with the ESRG Windows® tool set (usually, this means you must be the copyright holder).
  • You must maintain the paper or article in CVS at SourceForge.
  • You must be willing to assume zonal responsibility for the paper or article (react to comments, corrections, inquiries, etc.).
  • You must be willing to interact with CVS directly to make changes, corrections, etc.

If you'd like to contribute or assist, please send me (David T. Ashley) an e-mail!
