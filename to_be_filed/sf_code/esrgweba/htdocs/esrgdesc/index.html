What Is The ESRG, Anyway?

ESRG is short for Embedded Systems Research Group.  The ESRG is a collection of embedded software developers, managers, and researchers from many industries and areas of academia that have some interest in:

  • Fundamental research in embedded systems.
  • The organized open-source sharing of tools, source code, and libraries among software developers.
  • Development and dissemination of best practices.
  • Development and dissemination of lessons learned.
